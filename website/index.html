How it's summoned

You brief it. It does the rest.

i

Point it at your startup

A GitHub repo, your product site, docs — or all three. Send github.com/you/repo and yourcompany.com. A private repo it can't reach? Fine — it learns you from the rest.

ii

Tell it who to hunt

Plain language: find fintechs who'd need us. It infers your ideal customer and surfaces three verified fits to choose from.

iii

It delivers the campaign to your chat

Prototype link, walkthrough video, pitch deck, and a drafted email — with a live intel brief on the prospect while it builds.

iv

You approve. It drafts to Gmail. You close.

Amend anything in plain English. Nothing sends until you say so — the revenant hunts, you take the shot.
